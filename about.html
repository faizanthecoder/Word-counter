About CountIt4U

Who We Are

CountIt4U is a free online tool created by Faizan the Coder to make writing, editing, and counting text effortless. It helps you instantly calculate words, characters, and spaces — and even highlight or replace words in real time.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide simple, clean, and effective writing tools that help students, writers, and professionals save time and focus on creativity. CountIt4U is completely free and designed to be fast, accessible, and user-friendly.

Future Updates

We are continuously improving the platform. Planned features include reading-time estimation, paragraph count, and AI-based text suggestions. Stay tuned!
