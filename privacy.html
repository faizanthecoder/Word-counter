Privacy Policy

At CountIt4U, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. Our goal is to provide useful writing tools while keeping your information safe, secure, and transparent.

Information We Collect

CountIt4U does not collect, store, or share any personal text you enter. All text processing, including counting, analyzing, and corrections, happens directly in your browser. We never upload your content to our servers.

We use Google AdSense to display ads that support the free use of our tool. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can manage ad personalization by visiting your Google Ads settings.

External Links

Our site may contain links to external pages (like dictionary or language APIs). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

Data Security

All operations occur locally on your device using secure HTTPS protocols. We do not track or save any user-generated text or search history.
